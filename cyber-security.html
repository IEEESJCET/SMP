Cyber Security

The Protection of Internet Connected Systems

Cyber security also known as electronic security aims to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to defend the computer operating systems, networks and data from cyber attacks.

Cyber security means the protection of internet connected systems from cyber threats The cybersecurity analyst thoroughly understands cyberattacks, malware, and the behaviour of cybercriminals, and actively seeks to anticipate and prevent these attacks.

Cybersecurity is crucial because it safeguards all types of data against theft and loss. Main attraction of the event is the learners become a good mentor and they will get great idea on each stack

Student Mentorship Program(SMP) is a joint initiative by IEEE SIGHT SJCET AND IEEE WIE AG in association with IEEE SIGHT KERALA SECTION to find students capable and passionate in mentoring tech stacks to peers with intrest in tech. The prime aim of the programme is to serves as a platform for students who face credibility issues regarding their skills and experience, and also helps them realize, grow their true potential and dreams.
