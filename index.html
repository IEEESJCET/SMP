About The Event

About The Event

Proof of work or experience has refrained passionate students from upskilling themselves as potential mentors. Student Mentorship Program (SMP) is an initiative to put an end to such restraints. SMP focuses to improve and support the training skills of students as mentors in the key performance areas of teaching, research, and support service. After completing our training, the mentors will be send to educational institutions across the country to train their students on various stacks and assist them in creating their own projects. Through this programme, our prime aim is to generate a strong, passionate and qualified mentor workforce
